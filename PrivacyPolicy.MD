# Privacy Policy — Localhost No Cache

**Last updated: March 14, 2026**

## Data Collection

Localhost No Cache does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data or browsing information. The extension operates entirely on your local machine.

## What the Extension Does

The extension modifies request headers exclusively for traffic to `localhost` and `127.0.0.1`. This is done locally within Chrome using the `declarativeNetRequest` API. No request content, URLs, or responses are read or logged by the extension.

## Storage

The only data stored is a single boolean preference (whether the extension is enabled or disabled). This is saved locally in Chrome's extension storage and never leaves your device.

## Third Parties

This extension does not communicate with any external servers, analytics services, or third parties of any kind.

## Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, you can open an issue on the extension's repository.
